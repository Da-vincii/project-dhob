The hands behind your cloth.

Project dhoB is a living community of the people who wash, press, stitch, embroider, mend and revive the fabric of everyday life — brought together, and brought to your door.

Scroll to meet them

Behind every clean shirt, every fallen hem set right, every saree given its fall and pico, there is a pair of hands and a lifetime of craft. We are building the place where those hands are seen, valued, and just one tap away.

How it
comes together

From a tap on your phone to fresh, finished cloth — and fair work for the community.

01

Tell us the need

Wash, iron, dry clean, a falling hem, an invisible darn, an embroidered blouse — describe it, urgent or not.

02

Meet your makers

See the nearest artisans for that craft — their work, ratings and transparent prices, side by side.

03

Agree & we collect

Pick a maker or post a price. A dhoB rider picks up from your door at the slot you choose.

04

Crafted & returned

Back in 24–48 hours — or 3–4 on express. Done by a real person you can rate, thank and return to.

What we do

Every craft of cloth, in one community

🧺

Wash & press

Everyday laundry, ironing and folding by trusted neighbourhood dhobis and istriwalas.

🧵

Stitch & tailor

Alterations, repairs and bespoke tailoring from local darzis who know how cloth should fall.

🪡

Embroidery

Zardozi, chikankari and hand embroidery — heritage needlework for your most special pieces.

👞

Shoe & leather

The mochi's craft — resoling, polishing and reviving shoes, bags and leather goods.

⚡

Express, when urgent

Interview at 9, crease at 6? Post it, agree a price, get it back in 3–4 hours.

🏬

dhoB Atelier

Our own studios for guaranteed, premium results on delicate and treasured pieces.
